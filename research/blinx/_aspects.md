# _aspects.md — Master Aspect Taxonomy + Coverage Audit

The complete checklist of artistic aspects a scene can have. Classical canon
(7 elements + 10 principles, confirmed against standard sources) extended
with the categories games and cinema add: light & air, stage construction,
time & life, meaning & game language. 45 aspects total.

Status key: STRONG = torn down with crop evidence · PARTIAL = covered in
world files / dynamics but not object-level · MISSING = not yet analyzed.
Docs: _style.md (10 systems S1–S10), _objects.md (24 entries O1–O24),
_dynamics.md, _details.md, _rockbottom.md, _fundamentals.md, w1–w8 files.
